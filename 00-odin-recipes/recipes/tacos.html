Taco

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. The base is usually soft or crunchy corn or flour tortillas, filled with seasoned meat (such as ground beef, chicken, pork, or even fish) or plant-based proteins.

Tacos are then topped with fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, salsa, onions, and cilantro. They're versatile and can be customized to suit various tastes, making them a fun and flavorful meal enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Taco shell
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded lettuce

  5. Add toppings: Layer shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, salsa, onions, and cilantro (as desired).
